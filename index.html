Our Story

We build technology that serves people.

KMK Technologies was founded on a straightforward belief — that technology should work for everyone, not just the few. We exist to bridge the gap between the tools people need and the technology that currently exists to serve them.

We look at the daily friction people face — finding a pharmacy at midnight, spending hours tailoring a resume for a job that never replies — and we ask: why hasn't this been properly solved? Then we build the solution.

Our team brings together deep expertise in technology, product development, finance, and operations. We are practitioners who have spent years inside complex systems — and we know exactly where the gaps are.

Our Products

Two products. One mission.

One built for the streets of Pakistan. One built for job seekers everywhere. Both built to solve problems that existing tools have failed to solve properly.

Nishaan
Pakistan · Location Services

A location-based discovery app that connects people across Pakistan to essential services — pharmacies, hospitals, clinics, and more — exactly when they need them. No typing. No searching. Open the app and let Nishaan guide you.

  • Passive notifications as you drive past open services
  • Emergency mode — one tap to the nearest open pharmacy
  • Verified, community-maintained service data
  • Built for daily discovery and urgent situations alike

Workfluence
Global · Career Platform

An AI-powered career platform that starts with your entire life story and works backwards to every job you apply to. Tell us everything once — we write every resume, tailored to every role, every time. Your career guide. Not a resume builder.

  • One-time life profile — never write a resume again
  • AI compatibility score before you apply anywhere
  • Role-specific resumes, cover letters, and interview prep
  • Built for emerging market job seekers worldwide
